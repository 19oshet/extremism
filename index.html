How much do you know about extremism?

This is a quiz designed to tell you how much you really know about extremism and extremists and the current state of the world. This quiz also aims to show you some of your percieved biases about other people's beliefs.

You will be asked a series of multiple choice questions about extremism and also about your beliefs. As this is a sensitive topic there will be an option to skip any question you feel uncomfortable answering. Some questions may have multiple answers.

All information you input is not sent to us and is processed on your device. We do not collect any of your data. If you choose to share your score your answers are not shared or stored. The only information we collect is a view counter.

Remember: You can skip any question at any time.
